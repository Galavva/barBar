Bark Bar

Welcome and thank you for checking out our website. Bark Bar is a place for people who love two things. Bars and dogs! Whether you have a furry companion of your own or you just want to hang out with some dogs and similiar minded people then feel free to come on in check it out! We have a full alcoholic bar, a full coffee bar, and a full treat bar! Dog owners be sure to check out our For Puppers page to see what you need to bring your dog in.
